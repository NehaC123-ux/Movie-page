Finding Nemo

Finding Nemo" has all of the usual pleasures of the Pixar animation comedy and wackiness of "Toy Story" "Monsters Inc. Life." And it adds an unexpected beauty, a use of color and form that makes of those rare movies where I wanted to sit in the front row and let the images wash of field of vision. The movie takes place almost entirely under the sea, in the world of colorful tropical fish--the flora and fauna of a shallow warm-water shelf not far from Australia. The use of color, form and movement make the film a delight even apart from its story.
